YOU ARE THE FOCUS

Stride is designed to provide runners with a marketplace that not only has a great variety of products, but has the best product for you.

The simple three page structure allows users to either browse for shoes and filter their findings, or explore the main feed, which is constantly refreshed with exciting sneakers. The profile tab lets users see personal information as well as shoes they already own. The readily accessible 'Favorites' section allows users to check what sneakers are in their wishlist.
